Who I Am

About Me

A passionate researcher and educator specializing in intelligent computing systems, cloud infrastructure, and AI-driven solutions for real-world problems.

I hold a Ph.D. from the University of Hyderabad, with an M.Tech in Artificial Intelligence, and have spent over a decade contributing to academia and research institutions including the Reserve Bank of India's technology arm (IDRBT).

My work spans from QoS-aware cloud service composition to blockchain-based cyber-physical systems, with publications in top Elsevier, Springer, Wiley, and IEEE journals.
